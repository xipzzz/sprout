Rules & Reduce Motion
Tokens only
Durations + easings come from the scale — no hand-tuned one-offs per screen.
Gentle springs
Playful elements (Pip, plants, badges) overshoot softly; chrome uses plain ease-out.
Earned & brief
Bigger motion for rarer moments; every celebration ≤1.2s and tappable-through.
On-brand particles
Leaves / petals / droplets — never generic confetti.
Reduce Motion
When iOS Reduce Motion is on, particles + springs collapse to a simple fade — feedback kept, movement dropped. Haptics still fire.
Pip is alive
A small idle bob at rest, a celebratory pose on wins — the buddy never feels static.
